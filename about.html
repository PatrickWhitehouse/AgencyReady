About Patrick

Hi, I'm Patrick. You should already know the basics about me from the introduction paragraph on the home page! Well, if you didn't read that, my name is Patrick Whitehouse and I am currently a student at Manchester Metropolitan University. I'm in my final year studying Web Development (BSc).

I'm currently living in a town just outside of Manchester city centre named Denton. I've lived here all my life however I don't plan on living here forever! The world is a big place and I feel that I need to explore it before it's too late. On the topic of travelling, I've been very fortunate to have visited numerous of places. Once I have gained experience in the digital industry, I hope that I can take my skills overseas and reside in the US or Australia.

I have always been an outgoing individual. During my early teenage years, I was very fortunate to have been given the oppourtunity to play for a professional football club, Rochdale AFC. I was a footballer here for roughly two years before being released. After I was released I decided to experience a new sport and decided to compete in basketball. Again, I progressed quite far in this sport. Sporting achievements aside, I have won multiple academic awards at my high school. At school I was a hard working individual who wanted the best grades possible.

Before coming to university, I attended a sixth form college where I studied ICT. From a young age I have always had a gut instinct that IT will shape my future and be a key part in my life. My father too has always been in the IT industry (networking) and therefore I learnt a lot of IT skills at an early age from him.

After I've achieved my degree, I hope to find a career within the industry working as a front end web developer as this is where my passion lies. After I've grasped the basics and have a feel for what it is like working within the industry, I would like to experience working as a freelance developer.

In my free time, I enjoy attending car meets. This gives me an opportunity to build on my socialising skills and the chance to create new friendships. Majority of the time, I take my camera along to photograph the meet.
